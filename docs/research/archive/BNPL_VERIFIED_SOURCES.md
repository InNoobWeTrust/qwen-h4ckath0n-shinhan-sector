## RESEARCH LIMITATIONS & RECOMMENDATIONS

### Why BNPL Data is Difficult to Find:

1. **Limited Public Reporting:** Vietnam BNPL providers (MoMo, ZaloPay, GrabPay) do not publish detailed default rates or rejection statistics
2. **Regulatory Environment:** BNPL in Vietnam operates in a gray area without specific regulations requiring public disclosure
3. **Paywalled Reports:** Most industry research reports (IMARC, MarketsandMarkets, Statista) require paid subscriptions
4. **No Official Statistics:** Vietnam's central bank does not publish BNPL-specific metrics
5. **Fragmented Data:** Data exists in Vietnamese language reports but not in easily verifiable English-language sources

### Recommended Next Steps:

1. **Primary Research:** Consider conducting direct surveys or interviews with BNPL users in Vietnam
2. **Industry Contacts:** Reach out to MoMo, ZaloPay, GrabPay directly for partnership data
3. **Paid Research:** Purchase industry reports from:
   - IMARC Group (imarcgroup.com)
   - MarketsandMarkets (marketsandmarkets.com)
   - Statista (statista.com)
   - Ken Research
   - GlobalData
   
4. **Regulatory Filing:** Contact State Bank of Vietnam (SBV) directly for official statistics
5. **Academic Sources:** Check Vietnamese academic institutions (NIIS, Fulbright Vietnam) for research papers

### Alternative Verifiable Data Sources to Consider:

1. **VnEconomy** (vneconomy.vn) - Vietnamese economic news
2. **CafeF** (cafef.vn) - Vietnamese financial news  
3. **VietnamNet** (vietnamnet.vn) - Vietnamese news
4. **SBV Statistics** (sbv.gov.vn) - Official banking statistics
5. **GSMA Mobile Money** (gsma.com) - Mobile money data (may include some BNPL indicators)
