## Decision

- **Best pure hacker play**: `SB9`
- **Best data-fusion moat**: `SF12`
- **Best lower-risk alternative**: `SF11`

If the team wants the highest odds of winning with human cleverness instead of model theater, build one of those three.

## Known (model knowledge, unverified)

- The repo's earlier research consistently points to alternative underwriting and messy operational data fusion as the strongest defensible category.
- Use cases centered on POS, payroll, marketplace, wallet, compliance, and claims data have more room for handcrafted heuristics than use cases centered on generic chat, summarization, or transcription.
- Vietnam-specific workflows plausibly matter most in merchant payments, thin-file lending, payroll-linked credit, QR offer orchestration, and informal digital seller underwriting.

## Uncertain / needs verification

- Real hackathon access to POS, payroll, open-banking, marketplace, wallet, or claims data is not confirmed in this repo.
- Some use cases that score high on strategic moat may still be hard to demo if the team cannot mock credible data or partner APIs.
- The exact legal and operational boundaries for production deployment in Vietnam remain unverified here; this analysis is about hackathon advantage, not deployment approval.
